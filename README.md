# Luckerts Plugin Store Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of Luckerts Plugin Store's sales performance, offering insights into revenue trends, product performance, regional distribution, and customer purchasing patterns. The dashboard is designed to support data-driven decision-making for business growth and optimization.

## Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Sales**: 200 transactions tracked
- **Total Customers**: 20 unique customers
- **Total Amount**: ₦102,501 in revenue

### Visualizations

1. **Product Sales Analysis**
   - Horizontal bar chart showing top-selling products
   - Webcam leads with 31 sales, followed by Mouse (30) and Monitor (25)
   - Tracks 8 product categories including Router, Keyboard, Laptop, Printer, and Headset

2. **Regional Sales Distribution**
   - Donut chart displaying sales volume across Nigerian regions
   - Lagos dominates with 45.5% of total sales
   - Other regions include Abuja (25.5%), Port Harcourt (15.5%), and Kano (13.5%)

3. **Sales Performance by Day of Week**
   - Line chart tracking daily sales patterns
   - Identifies peak sales days (Day 5) and low-performance days (Day 3)
   - Helps optimize staffing and inventory management

4. **Top 5 Highest Purchasing Customers**
   - Bar chart highlighting most valuable customers
   - Ada leads with 18 purchases, followed by Chioma (17), Ethan (15), Femi (15), and Linda (14)
   - Supports customer relationship management and loyalty programs

### Interactive Filters

The dashboard includes multiple slicers for dynamic data exploration:
- **Category**: Filter by Accessories or Electronics
- **Segment**: Corporate, Retail, or SMB
- **Week Type**: Weekday or Weekend analysis

## Technical Specifications

### Tools Used
- **Power BI Desktop**: Dashboard development and visualization
- **Data Source**: Sales transaction database (`.pbix` file)
- **Currency**: Nigerian Naira (₦)

### Data Model
The dashboard analyzes sales data with the following dimensions:
- Products (Webcam, Mouse, Monitor, Router, Keyboard, Laptop, Printer, Headset)
- Regions (Lagos, Abuja, Kano, Port Harcourt)
- Customers (20 unique customers tracked)
- Time (Day of week analysis)
- Categories (Accessories, Electronics)
- Segments (Corporate, Retail, SMB)

## Key Insights

1. **Product Performance**: Webcams and mice are the top revenue drivers
2. **Regional Concentration**: Lagos accounts for nearly half of all sales
3. **Customer Loyalty**: Top 5 customers show consistent purchasing patterns
4. **Sales Patterns**: Significant variation by day of week suggests opportunity for targeted promotions
